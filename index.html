Personal Information

I am Rowela G. Perpetua, student from Technological University of the Philippines - Manila. I am currently a third year student finishing a bachelor degree in Information System. I was born and raised in the city of Manila somewhere in District 2. I entered college with a tiniest idea of how my program would turn out. Of course, I am aware of the relevance of it in the IT field, I just did not have enough idea of what would it take to get there, to be able to achieve the destination we are aiming for. As I progress in this program, I realized how challenging it is to comply with everything needed in this course, in spite of that, I learned to enjoy and have fun in this field even in the most difficult times. Day by day, I find myself learning and slowly figuring out which line of work will work best with my interest.
